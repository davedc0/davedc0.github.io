Tina and Rook

Tina and Rook is a solo game project that I am currently working on. The game is a puzzle adventure game that is inspired by the like of Scooby Doo, Deponia, Professor Layton and Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective. The game follow two main character named Tina and Rook as they do chores to get cookies.

Link to Game here

The player must go around and find objects that they can use to solve puzzles in the game. Like in the game Deponia the player also can combine 2 items to create a new item that they can use. By dragging items into the screen they can use the item to interact with the environment. The story is very simple and cute, it's not heavy and can be enjoyed by anyone like a slice of life manga.

The game manage to get more than 7000 players and more than 100 good reviews.

Art Work

The current game act as a demo and a test for a longer game with longer and more impactful story. The story will resolve on Tina and Rook adventure on a small village that is haunted by a ghost. By exploring the village the player must uncover the dark secret that's hidden in that village. The story of the game will take a lot of inspiration from Scooby-Doo, Detective Conan, Ace Attorney, and Professor Layton, where while the story is quite heavy sometimes, but didn't have too much drama that makes the story too adulterous. That way the story will be able to be enjoyed by kids alike.
